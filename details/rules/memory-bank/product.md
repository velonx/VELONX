# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
VELONX is a comprehensive student learning and collaboration platform that connects students with mentors, projects, events, and educational resources. The platform gamifies the learning experience through XP systems, badges, achievements, and leaderboards while fostering community engagement through discussion rooms, groups, and social features.

## Value Proposition
- **Unified Learning Hub**: Centralized platform for students to access mentorship, participate in events, collaborate on projects, and discover resources
- **Gamified Engagement**: XP system, badges, achievements, streaks, and leaderboards motivate continuous learning and participation
- **Community-Driven**: Discussion rooms, community groups, posts, comments, and social features enable peer-to-peer learning
- **Career Development**: Mock interviews, mentor sessions, internship/job opportunities, and project showcases prepare students for careers
- **Personalized Experience**: Customizable profiles, notification preferences, referral system, and activity tracking

## Key Features

### Learning & Mentorship
- **Mentor Sessions**: Book 1-on-1 sessions with industry mentors, rate and review sessions
- **Mock Interviews**: Apply for technical, behavioral, DSA, and system design interview practice
- **Resources Library**: Access curated articles, videos, courses, books, tools, and documentation with PDF support
- **Resource Tracking**: XP rewards for first-time resource visits

### Events & Activities
- **Event Management**: Hackathons, workshops, and webinars with registration, capacity limits, and deadlines
- **Event Analytics**: Track registration closures, failed attempts, and attendance patterns
- **Calendar Integration**: View upcoming events with meeting links and location details

### Projects & Collaboration
- **Project Showcase**: Create and display projects with tech stack, GitHub/live URLs, and difficulty levels
- **Hall of Fame**: Completed projects featured with completion timestamps
- **Team Collaboration**: Multi-member projects with role assignments
- **Project Categories**: Web dev, mobile, AI/ML, data science, DevOps, design

### Gamification System
- **XP & Leveling**: Earn XP from projects, events, mentorship, community contributions, streaks, and referrals
- **Badges**: Unlock badges across categories (project, event, mentor, community, streak, milestone, special)
- **Achievements**: Track milestones like first project, event streaks, collaborations
- **Leaderboards**: Daily, weekly, monthly, and all-time rankings
- **Streaks**: Daily login tracking with current and longest streak records
- **Referral System**: Unique referral codes with XP rewards for successful referrals

### Community Features
- **Discussion Rooms**: Public/private chat rooms with moderators and member management
- **Community Groups**: Interest-based groups with posts, messaging, and join requests
- **Social Posts**: Create posts with images, links, reactions (like, love, insightful, celebrate)
- **Comments & Threads**: Nested comment system with upvotes/downvotes
- **Social Graph**: Follow/unfollow users, view followers and following
- **Moderation Tools**: Mute users, delete content, pin posts, flag content, assign moderators
- **User Controls**: Block users, manage privacy settings

### Notifications & Communication
- **Real-time Notifications**: Info, success, award, event, warning, comment, reaction, mention alerts
- **Email Preferences**: Granular control over event reminders, mentor notifications, project updates, weekly digests
- **Community Notifications**: Comments, reactions, mentions, group updates, moderation actions
- **Unread Tracking**: Badge counts for unread notifications

### Career Opportunities
- **Internships & Jobs**: Browse active opportunities with company details, requirements, and application links
- **Opportunity Management**: Admin-posted positions with status tracking (active, closed, draft)

### Admin & Analytics
- **User Management**: Role-based access (student, admin)
- **Event Registration Analytics**: Track closure reasons, capacity metrics, time-to-closure
- **Audit Logging**: Security event tracking for logins, resource access, and user actions
- **Content Moderation**: Review and manage user-generated content

## Target Users

### Primary Users (Students)
- Computer science and engineering students seeking mentorship and project experience
- Self-taught developers looking for structured learning paths and community support
- Career switchers preparing for technical interviews and building portfolios
- Students seeking internships and entry-level job opportunities

### Secondary Users (Mentors)
- Industry professionals offering guidance and expertise
- Experienced developers conducting mock interviews
- Technical leads sharing knowledge through workshops and webinars

### Administrators
- Platform managers overseeing content, events, and user management
- Community moderators ensuring quality discussions and safe environment
- Analytics reviewers tracking engagement and platform health

## Use Cases

1. **Student Onboarding**: Sign up with referral code, complete profile, earn initial XP, unlock first badges
2. **Event Participation**: Register for hackathon, receive confirmation email, attend event, earn XP and badges
3. **Mentor Booking**: Browse mentors by expertise, book session, receive confirmation, attend meeting, leave review
4. **Project Collaboration**: Create project, invite team members, update progress, mark complete, showcase in Hall of Fame
5. **Community Engagement**: Join discussion rooms, create posts, comment on discussions, react to content, earn community badges
6. **Resource Learning**: Browse resources by category, access PDFs/links, earn XP on first visit, track learning progress
7. **Career Preparation**: Apply for mock interview, practice with mentor, browse job opportunities, build portfolio
8. **Gamification Loop**: Complete activities, earn XP, level up, unlock badges, climb leaderboards, maintain streaks
9. **Referral Growth**: Share referral code, invite friends, earn XP when referees complete activities
10. **Moderation**: Moderators manage content, mute disruptive users, pin important posts, maintain community guidelines
